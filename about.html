I am Edward Lim.

I am a User Experience Designer based in San Francisco Bay Area with a love for elegant and powerful visuals. I also have a background in Art Direction, and knowledge in Front-end development. What I bring to the table is solving user-centered problems through research to actionable insight, and from there to branding, strong concept ideation, information architecture, and strategic visual execution.

I grew up in San Francisco Bay Area, studied advertising and graduated from Art Center College of Design in Pasadena, Ca. After college, I ran the family business and also had design jobs on the side. The truth is, I love design, and have a natural passion for design, art, photography, architecture, tech, music, movies, and food. I have always been so curious about how design influences and drives our culture in such powerful ways. Looking at my portfolio, it lacked digital presence and decided to study UX/UI Design and Front-end Development.

Running a business helped me create great experiences for customers as well as employees. It taught me how to be a good leader by dealing with multiple clients, employees, and creating great experiences for customers.
I am currently looking to join a team of passionate designers where I can build great products where all members of the team can learn, grow, and thrive together.

In my free time, I enjoy playing golf, traveling, watching movies, and grabbing a beer.
